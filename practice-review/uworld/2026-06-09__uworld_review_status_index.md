## Naming rule going forward

Examples:

- `2026-06-09__uworld_test_2_id_410734662__completed_review_guide.md`
- `2026-06-10__uworld_test_3__in_progress_review_notes.md`
- `2026-06-15__uworld_test_2__retake_verified_update.md`

Do not rename or delete historical files solely because their old status word is stale. Record the corrected status in a dated index or a new versioned file.
