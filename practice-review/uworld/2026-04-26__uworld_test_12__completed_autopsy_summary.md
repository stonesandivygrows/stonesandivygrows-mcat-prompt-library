## Highest-yield durable corrections

- A mutagen changes genetic material; do not automatically translate it into cell-cycle progression.
- Empty vector is a negative control for the transfection procedure without the functional insert.
- A dNTP can be incorporated because it has a 3′-OH on its sugar and a triphosphate on the 5′ carbon; the growing strand attacks the incoming alpha phosphate.
- cDNA is complementary DNA synthesized from RNA by reverse transcriptase.
- A probe is complementary to the target sequence.
- Blood contacts endothelial cells inside blood vessels.
- Oligodendrocytes myelinate the CNS; Schwann cells myelinate the PNS.
- More waves in a fixed time interval means higher frequency.
- Lower pH means higher H+ concentration.
- Hydrostatic pressure pushes fluid out of capillaries; oncotic pressure pulls fluid into capillaries.
- Enveloped viruses use host synthesis and secretory-pathway machinery and commonly leave by budding; lysosomes are not the central release route.
