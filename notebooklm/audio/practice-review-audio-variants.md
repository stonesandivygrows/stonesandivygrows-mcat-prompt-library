# NotebookLM Practice Review Audio Variants

These are lighter and deeper NotebookLM variants so you can match the audio prompt to either one passage or a full section.

---

## 1. CARS Single-Passage Quick Audio Variant

```text
Use the main CARS Practice Review Audio Prompt, but make the scope ONLY one passage.

Additional rules:
- Keep the episode focused on one passage only.
- Spend more time on the passage’s main idea, paragraph roles, and author tone.
- Diagnose exactly where my comprehension became wrong if it did.
- Keep the review practical and short enough to feel like a post-passage tutoring session.
- End with 3 short coaching rules and 2 to 3 mini practice questions based on the same weakness.

Begin exactly with:
“CARS Single-Passage Review”
```

## 3. CARS Full-Section Deep Audio Variant

```text
Use the main CARS Practice Review Audio Prompt, but treat the upload as a full-section or multi-passage performance review.

Additional rules:
- Track how my comprehension, timing, and decision-making changed from early to middle to late passages.
- Explain whether fatigue, rushing, panic, or confidence changes appeared across the section.
- Include a distinct section called “Section Progression Pattern.”
- Include a distinct section called “If This Were Test Day,” where you explain what I should have done at each important turning point in the section.
- Build a realistic section-level plan, not just passage-level advice.
- End with a concrete pacing and recovery plan for the next full CARS section.

Begin exactly with:
“CARS Full-Section Review”
```
