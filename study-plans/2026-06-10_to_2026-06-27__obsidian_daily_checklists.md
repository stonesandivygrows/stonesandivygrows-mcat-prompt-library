**Window:** 2026-06-10 to 2026-06-27

**Current-cycle full-lengths completed:** AAMC Practice Exam 1 + UWorld Exam 1

## Rules

- Progress = completed assignments, not hours studied.
- Questions + review are the spine.
- CARS = gym workout, not completion checklist.
- MCATalyst = targeted repair + recall output.
- Equations/units = active recall pages, not passive rereading.
- Mind maps = struggle-triggered only.
- Anki app is optional; memory can happen by app, paper, iPad, sticky notes, posters, physical flashcards, drawings, or infographics.
- No catch-up debt after reduced days.

- **P0:** Full-length / full-length review

- **P4:** Daily question / repair block
- **P5:** Flexible memory set / MCATalyst Anki content
