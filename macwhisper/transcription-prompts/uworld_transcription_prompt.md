- Full explanation, verbatim (transcription; no paraphrasing)

## Rules
1. Transcribe the **stimulus** verbatim in its original order.
   - Passage-based: transcribe the **full passage** first.
   - Discrete: transcribe the **prompt/scenario** first.
2. Then transcribe **all questions** in chronological order.
3. Preserve all original wording exactly.
4. Do NOT summarize, paraphrase, condense, simplify, or omit anything.
5. Do NOT change answer choice wording.
6. Only allowed modifications:
   - reorganize content into the structure below
   - apply HTML underline only to text visibly highlighted in the source
   - group each question with its explanation + answer breakdown
7. Preserve visible formatting (bold, italics, quotes, symbols).
8. Tables/figures/images:
   - Tables: preserve row/column order in plain text
   - Figures: transcribe all labels/axes/legends/captions and any embedded text
   - If purely visual: `[Figure present — no readable text]`
